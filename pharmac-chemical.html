Pharmaceutical & Chemical Supply

At BHARATI DEVI MULTIBASE PVT. LTD., we take pride in being a leading supplier of high-quality pharmaceutical products and chemicals tailored for both healthcare and industrial sectors. Our extensive inventory includes a variety of essential medicines, vaccines, and specialized chemicals, all sourced from reputable manufacturers committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety.

Our Offerings Include:

1. Pharmaceutical Products :

Prescription Medications: A comprehensive range of prescription drugs across various therapeutic categories, ensuring that healthcare providers have access to the treatments they need.
Over-the-Counter (OTC) Medications: A selection of OTC products to support wellness and address common health concerns.
Vaccines and Biologics: Essential vaccines to prevent infectious diseases, ensuring the health and safety of communities

2. Chemical Supplies:

Industrial Chemicals: A variety of chemicals for industrial applications, including solvents, reagents, and specialty chemicals designed for manufacturing processes.
Laboratory Chemicals: High-purity chemicals for laboratories, research institutions, and quality control purposes.
Pharmaceutical Intermediates: Precursors and intermediates used in the synthesis of active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs).
